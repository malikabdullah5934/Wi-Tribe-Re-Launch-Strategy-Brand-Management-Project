# 🌐 Wi-Tribe Re-Launch Strategy & Brand Management Project

This project presents a complete **data-driven rebranding and relaunch strategy** for Wi-Tribe, a former broadband provider in Pakistan. The analysis combines **brand management, market research, sentiment analysis, financial modeling, and UI/UX prototyping** to redesign Wi-Tribe as a modern internet service brand.

---

## 🚀 Project Overview

The project focuses on identifying the reasons behind Wi-Tribe’s market failure and developing a strategic relaunch plan capable of competing with modern ISPs such as PTCL FlashFiber, Nayatel, and StormFiber.

The relaunch strategy emphasizes:

* Reliable connectivity
* Affordable broadband solutions
* Modern brand positioning
* Customer-focused digital experience

---

## 📊 Phase 1: Forensic Failure & Market Audit

A detailed investigation was conducted to identify the root causes of Wi-Tribe’s decline.

### Key Areas Analyzed

* Sentiment analysis (2015–2020)
* Customer feedback & complaint trends
* Competitor benchmarking
* Reliability & pricing comparison
* Market growth and FTTH opportunities
* Regression and trend analysis

### Major Findings

* Outdated WiMAX technology
* Poor customer support
* Weak market adaptation
* Limited coverage expansion
* Declining public trust

---

## 🎯 Phase 2: Relaunch Strategy

A complete relaunch framework was designed for modern broadband consumers.

### 🔹 Brand Identity

* **Tagline:** *“Your Golden Connection to Tomorrow”*
* Modernized logo & brand style guide
* Youthful and tech-focused tone

### 🔹 Target Segments

* Students
* Freelancers & remote workers
* Tech professionals
* Families

### 🔹 Marketing Strategy (4Ps)

* Product redesign (5G-ready + FTTH)
* Competitive pricing plans
* Expansion into underserved cities
* Digital-first promotional campaigns

---

## 🎨 UI/UX & Prototyping

Two interactive prototype variants (A/B) were designed and tested to measure:

* CTR (Click-Through Rate)
* Conversion Rate
* User Engagement
* ROI & CAC

### ✅ Final Result

Variant A achieved:

* Higher engagement
* Better conversion rate
* Stronger ROI
* Improved customer interaction
